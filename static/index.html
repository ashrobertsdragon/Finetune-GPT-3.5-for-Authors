Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does LoreBinders do?

LoreBinders transforms your manuscript into a detailed and organized story bible. It helps you keep track of characters, locations, and narrative elements, making your writing process more structured and efficient.

How do I use LoreBinders?

Simply upload your manuscript in one of the supported file formats (epub, docx, PDF, or text), specify the details you want to include in your story bible, and submit. You'll receive a comprehensive PDF of your LoreBinder within a reasonable time frame.

How does LoreBinders organize my manuscript?

LoreBinders categorizes and structures the key elements of your story—such as characters and locations—based on the details you provide, creating an organized story bible.

Will AI be trained on my story?

No, your story remains yours. We use AI to analyze and organize your manuscript, but your content is not used for training the AI or shared in any way.

What about the privacy and security of my manuscript?

Your manuscript's security and confidentiality are paramount. We ensure that your data is encrypted and securely stored, accessible only to you.

Can I customize what goes into my LoreBinder?

Definitely! You can choose the specific elements and details you want to include in your story bible, tailoring it to suit your project's needs.

Is there a limit to the size of the manuscript I can upload?

For optimal processing, we have size guidelines. Files must be no larger than 2MB. Note: PDFs where the pages are scanned images not converted to text are not supported at this time.

What if I have multiple projects?

Our bundle offers are ideal for authors with multiple projects. Purchase LoreBinders at a discounted rate, and use them whenever you're ready—they never expire.
